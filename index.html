CCRC Souvenire Shop
There are two rivers named Li. One flows through London, the other through the Chinese city of Yangshuo. The resonance between these two geographically distant entities sparked reflections on the mechanics of cultural intersections. Are such intersections truly mere consequences of cultural expansion, or do they emerge long before — spontaneously and organically, rooted in the shared nature of humanity?
1.
1.1. The Center for Cross-Cultural Studies is hereby established as a mental center investigating the phenomenon of cultural intersections.
1.2. The names of the Center for Cross-Cultural Research, Центр Кросскультурных Исследований, are equivalent within the scope of the Center's activities.
1.3. Everything that follows is temporary and subject to change.
